About Adam

Hello, I'm Adam! As a skilled full stack web engineer, I possess expertise in both backend development and UI/UX design. I have a proven track record of creating robust full stack web applications that effectively address business challenges and cater to organizational needs. From optimizing website navigation and layout to facilitating seamless communication with digital assets stored in databases, I handle it all.

In the frontend realm, I excel in JavaScript, React, and HTML/CSS, enabling me to deliver intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. On the backend, I have extensive experience coding with Node.js (Express), Postgres, and MongoDB, ensuring efficient data management and server-side functionality.

However, during the lockdown, I discovered a passion for web development and took the opportunity to further enhance my skills. Over the past three years, I diligently pursued my learning journey through various Codecademy courses, consistently completing projects and conquering challenges in both frontend and backend development.

Driven by my newfound passion, I have decided to pursue a permanent career in coding. To showcase my abilities, I have developed a personal website that features links to my projects and GitHub page, providing you with a comprehensive overview of my work.

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing how my skills and experience can contribute to your team's success.
