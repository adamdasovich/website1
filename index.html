About Adam

Hi, I’m Adam! I'm a full stack web engineer with experience in backend development and in UI/UX design. I have created full stack web apps that can help organizations address business challenges and meet their needs. I manage everything from website navigation and layout to enabling efficient communication with a company's digital assets within its database(s). My skills in the frontend include JavaScript, React, and of course HTML/CSS. My expertise in back-end include coding with Node.js (Express), Postgres and MongoDB.

Prior to Covid, I was a consulting engineer to a few junior mining companies. During the lockdown, I started dabbling with web-dev and found it very interesting. To provide some structure, and direction to my learning, I enrolled in various Codecademy courses over the last 2 years as a guide.

I have built many projects and completed most of Codecademy Challenges for both frontend and backend development. As I continue this path, I have decided that I would like to code on a permanent basis. As such, I have put together this website that has links to projects and my github page.
